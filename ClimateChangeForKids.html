What is Climate Change?
Climate change is the fast and dangerous change in climate.
Climate is the average weather and tempeture in a place over a long period of time.
In our modern society, we brun fossil fuels in our daily life for thing like cars,
factories, homes and so much more.
When these fossil fuels are burned, they let out green house gases like carbon dioxide that linger in the atmosphere
These green house gases trap the sunlight that enters the earth and makes the earth hotter and hotter.
